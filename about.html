About Spokane Cyber Cup

We are a small not-for-profit group focused on developing cybersecurity talent in the greater Spokane Washington area. Our first event was a collegiate and high school capture the flag hacking competition, “Spokane Mayor's Cup" hosted at Gonzaga University in February 2019 with 60 students and 8 coaches. In 2025, the event housed over 260 students and 40 coaches from a variety of different schools in the Pacific Northwest. We hope to continue the event to support the growth of more students.


Hosting location by year:

  • 2019: Gonzaga University
  • 2020: Spokane Falls Community College
  • 2022: Eastern Washington University
  • 2023: Gonzaga University
  • 2024: Eastern Washington University
  • 2025: Spokane Falls Community College
  • 2026 (upcoming year): Gonzaga University


The next event will be hosted at Gonzaga University on February 7th, 2026.

We are dedicated to continuing this regional competition and expanding our efforts to attract potential cybersecurity professionals.
