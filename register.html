Register for the 2026 Spokane Cyber Cup.

New to cybersecurity or hacking?

This event is for you! This event is tailored towards learning, with coaches walking around helping you with challenges.


A veteran of cybersecurity?

This event is also for you! Take the next step in your cybersecurity journey. This is a wonderful place to learn about the industry from cybersecurity professionals and a fantastic networking opportunity.


You can sign up as a team or as an individual. If you sign up as an individual, we will find you a team to play on.


There is a hard capacity on the amount of students. If this number of signups is met then we will have to close registration early and will create a weight list. Otherwise, it will remain open until the morning of the event. Sign up while spots are still available!
